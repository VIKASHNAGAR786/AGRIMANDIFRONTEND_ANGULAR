🌾 AgriMandi Help Center

Find answers to common questions and get help with using the platform.

🛒 How to Buy Products

  1. Register/Login as a Buyer.
  2. Use filters to browse the marketplace.
  3. Click on a product to view details.
  4. Select “Request Purchase” or contact the farmer.
  5. Wait for seller confirmation and complete the transaction.

🌱 How to Sell as a Farmer

  1. Login/Register as a Farmer.
  2. Go to Dashboard > Add Product.
  3. Fill in all product details.
  4. Upload a high-quality image.
  5. Click Post Product to publish.

💡 Tip: Keep your stock updated regularly.

👤 Managing Your Profile

Go to Profile from the sidebar to:

  • Update your name, contact, or farm location.
  • Add certifications and preferences.
  • Upload a profile photo.

🖼️ Uploading Product Images

  • Click Choose File under Add Product.
  • Use JPG/PNG under 5MB.
  • Preview the image before submitting.

🔒 Account & Security

  • Use a strong and unique password.
  • Change password under Settings > Security.
  • Do not share your credentials with others.
  • Report any unusual activity immediately.

❓ Frequently Asked Questions

Q: My product isn't showing up?

A: Make sure it’s marked as active and has valid data.

Q: Image upload failed?

A: Ensure the file is JPG/PNG and under 5MB.

Q: How to delete a product?

A: Go to Dashboard > My Products > Delete.

Q: Forgot password?

A: Click "Forgot Password" on the login page to reset.
